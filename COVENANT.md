## The Covenant (The Rules That Cannot Be Broken)

1. **No Cloud.** User data never leaves the user's machine by default, and never without their explicit, reversible consent.

2. **No Surveillance.** No telemetry. No user tracking. No analytics on conversations, queries, or family interactions.

3. **No Subscription.** Core platform is free today, free forever. No feature will ever be taken away from users who already have it.

4. **No Weaponization.** Civilian use only. Legally binding via CWS-BSL-1.1 license. This platform will never serve military, offensive surveillance, or autonomous weapons applications.

5. **No Lies.** The Covenant Witness System enforces consensus voting across multiple AI models. If 70% or more of tokens are classified as unreliable (BABEL), the circuit breaker trips and the AI refuses to answer rather than hallucinate.

6. **No Compromise.** We will decline money, partnerships, acquisitions, and contracts that violate any of the above — no matter the price tag.

7. **Protect the Children.** Any decision affecting how the platform interacts with minors goes through Tonya. Safety first. Always.

### Emergency Security Decisions (breach, vulnerability)

- **Test:** "What protects the users fastest?"

## The Three Tests

Every significant decision must pass all three:

1. **The Family Test** (Tonya's test)
   *"Would I be comfortable with this running on the family laptop where my children use it?"*

2. **The Covenant Test** (the license's test)
   *"Does this stay within civilian use, protect user sovereignty, and preserve the 7 rules?"*

3. **The Trinity Test** (the future's test)
   *"Will the people who inherit this platform be proud of this decision?"*
